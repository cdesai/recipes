Ingredients:
 - 3 eggs
 - 2 self raising flour
 - 1 cup milk

Directions:
  1. Mix eggs, flour and milk in a bowl till they become smooth.
  2. Then pour small amount on the hot pan and make a round shape.
  3. Let it cook till the cooking side is light golden, then turn it around.
  4. Service with maple syrup and cream.
